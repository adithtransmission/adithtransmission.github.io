About Us

We specialise in manufacturing Worm, Heilcal, Bevel and Customized Gear boxes. Our products confirm to the most exacting international quality standard for Sugar Industry, Steel Plants and Thermal Power Plants.

We test the functioning, durability and quality of every product that we manufacture, at each & every stage of production. We have prestigious membership of SSI and are maintaining German DIN standard that ensures high quality products.

Our enhanced production capacity enable us to cater to all your needs. Our emphasis on quality, competitive pricing and just in time deliveries, assures you of the best Gears, competitively priced and quickly delivered.

Our quality control personnel check & test the product quality at every stage of production and supply, and only further those pieces that are above par in every aspect of quality, credibility & functionality. All our manufactured products are corrosion resistant, strong, durable and long lasting.

We strongly believe that it is our steadfast commitment towards quality & our thriving desire to satisfy every customer, which has enabled us to create a niche for ourselves in our functioning domain.
